Glossary

  • Role: short description of what a you do. E.g. build features for the iOS application.
  • Title: the convenient name for a person who can fullfill one or more roles. Needs to be flexible for roles. E.g. Senior Software Engineer.
  • Discipline: your area of general expertise. E.g. Software, QA. Not platform or product specific.
  • Vertical: a business unit that is part of the Scout24 group. These are: Immobilien Scout GmbH, Consumer Services, AutoScout24 GmbH or "OneScout" department (e.g. Product Engineering).
  • Department: logical group of teams or a segment. E.g. Developer, Private, CXP.
