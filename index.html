About

This document is intended to provide clarity to the engineers and their managers in Scout24 as to what their job title means and to provide a basis for discussions for career development. Along with Promotion Endorsements, the Levels & Definitions should improve the fairness of promotions and hiring no matter what team or department an engineer is in.

If you're new to this, it is worth reading this How To page and the Glossary. All the pages in Levels & Definitions are formatted to be printable. If you'd like to suggest a change, please read the Readme.
