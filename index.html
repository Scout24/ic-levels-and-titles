About

This document is intended to provide clarity to the engineers and their managers in Scout24 as to what their job title means and to provide a basis for discussions on career development. Along with Promotion Endorsements, the Levels & Definitions should help provide fairness and consistency in promotions and hiring no matter what team or department an engineer is in.

Expected Behaviours by Competency

  • Influence
  • Collaboration & Communication
  • Functional Ability
  • Quality & Reliability
  • Business Impact

If you're new to this, it is worth reading this How To page and the Glossary. All the pages in Levels & Definitions are formatted to be printable. If you'd like to suggest a change, please read the Readme.
